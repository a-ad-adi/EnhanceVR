Kyrin Pollock & Matt Gill Tedx 2016

Sophomores Kyrin Pollock and Matthew Gill will be speaking together about Enhance VR, their virtual reality start-up that is housed under the Binghamton University department of research and entrepreneurship. Their talk will focus both on explaining their business and research, and debunking what they believe are negative associations people have with virtual reality technology.

Enhance Team Takes Best Hardware Hack

February 13, 2016

Team members from Enhance VR won the "Best Hardware Hack" at the 24 hr hackathon competition at Binghamton University (Hack BU). They won with their "Beats-Vest", a hacked vest with transducers in it to make vibrations in a user's chest. It can make the bass feeling like at aconcert or the roar of an engine in a race car.
